Observe My Ways
The inspiration for this illustration is Proverbs 23:26, which reads "My son, give me your heart, and let your eyes observe my ways." I intend to portray the closeness of scrutiny that humans need to have of their own lives if they are to call themselves examples for others.

Tools: Adobe Illustrator
